    To unlock this trophy, you must first complete eleven Side Quests. Afterwards, start the Main Quest THE LOOMING SHADOW. The trophy will unlock when starting that Quest. You can keep track of your Quest progress in-game by opening the General Menu with Touch Pad and going to Quests. At this point in the game, all the Side Quests must have been unlocked.
    作为此奖杯的条件，你需要首先完成11个支线任务。之后开启主线任务“朦胧暗影”，此奖杯将在任务开始时解锁（准确的说是在“醒来”后解锁）。你可以通过在游戏中按触摸板打开菜单进入任务列表检索任务过程。在此时（终章前），所有支线均为解锁状态了。（也就是说你需要去地图上的部落接问号，然后以下11个支线任务做完，再开启终章任务即满足条件）

    3.A Moments Peace 片刻的安宁
    4.A Daughter’s Vengeance 女儿的复仇
    5.Sun and Shadow 太阳与阴影
    6.Honor the Fallen 死者为大
    7.Sunstone Rock 太阳岩
    8.Hunting For the Lodge - Errand Side Quest （差事）猎人之家的狩猎
    9.Hunter’s Blind - Available after completing "Hunting For the Lodge" 猎人的埋伏处（8完成后接到）
    10.Deadliest Game - Errand Side Quest - Available after completing "Hunter's Blind" （差事）致命猎物（9完成后接到）
